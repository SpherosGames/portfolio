Stellar Repair

General information


It was a game made for my school’s game jam. This was in the first year, so also my first time participating in this jam. But not my first game jam, I’ve done quite a bit more in the past. Using that knowledge, I lead a team of first- and second-year students. It’s a 3d game with an orthographic view, meaning it looks 2D. It’s a puzzle game where the vacuum of space is your enemy.

What I did


Dialogue system:
The game features a simple dialogue system, with a text animation and skipping.

Door system:
Interact with levers to open and close doors. I made that with an animator.

Player movement:
With a sprinting feature

Oxygen system:
A simple bar that goes down and when it drops to zero, the player dies. You can refill it by standing in an oxygen filled area or picking up oxygen canisters.
